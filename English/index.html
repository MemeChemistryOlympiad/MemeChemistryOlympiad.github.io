Let us all face it:

Chemistry. Is. Boring. As. Fudge.

Almost every person who studied Chemistry knows that feeling of boredom upon encountering some
of the Chemistry problems, reactions, mechanisms, syntheses…
It seems obvious that this must be part of the learning process, and overcoming the boredom is considered normal. It is not true! Our team of students and mentors worked hard to create the ultimate solution-


International Meme Chemistry Olympiad

-The fusion of Chemistry and memes.


The International Meme Chemistry Olympiad (MemeChO) is a way for individuals and teams from any age group to test out
their creativity, problem-solving and collaboration in such an interesting Science field as Chemistry.

Our team of problem authors carefully engineered the whole competition so that the seemingly boring and useless Chemistry problems appear funny, interesting and challenging.

There are absolutely no limitations, every person may participate!

We can guarantee you that the few hours every two months you and your team spend participating in the Olympiad will be spent in a productive and funny manner.

Last, but not least, participation is totally free, and even rewarding to the best of the participants, so why not give it a shot?

Choose Your League

In order to make problems solvable yet still challenging for every Participant, we invented Leagues!
Leagues come in different varieties, but the only thing that changes is the difficulty level of the problems-the fun remains.
Amateur League

Fit For Amateurs

  • Teams up to three and Solo Members
  • Has its own Ranking system
  • High-school level problems
  • Ideal for high school students and Chemistry enthusiasts without a proper background
  • No college students or chemists allowed!
